–# Privacy Policy

**oura-briefing** is an open-source personal CLI tool that connects to your Oura account to generate a morning health briefing.

## Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, or share your data with any third parties.

- Your Oura health data is fetched directly from the Oura API to your local machine
- Your data never leaves your computer except for requests to the Oura API and Anthropic API
- OAuth tokens are stored locally in `~/.config/oura-briefing/tokens.json`
- No analytics, no tracking, no external servers

## Third-party Services

- **Oura API** — to fetch your health metrics. Subject to [Oura's Privacy Policy](https://ouraring.com/privacy-policy).
- **Anthropic API** — to generate your briefing text. Subject to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).
